Our Story

Born from a passion for exceptional food

From Kitchen to City

Founded in 2024, Savoro began as a small kitchen experiment between friends who believed food delivery could be so much more than fast — it could be unforgettable. What started as weekend pop-up dinners quickly grew into a movement, as word spread about our commitment to bold flavors, locally sourced ingredients, and dishes prepared with genuine care.

Today, Savoro partners with over 80 local farms and artisan producers to bring you a menu that changes with the seasons. Every dish is crafted from scratch, every ingredient chosen with intention, and every delivery made with the same passion that started it all in that tiny kitchen. We're not just delivering food — we're delivering a promise that great meals can reach your door without compromising on quality, sustainability, or soul.
