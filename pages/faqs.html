🍽️ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering from Savoro

How do I place an order on Savoro?
Simply browse our menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay via credit/debit card, UPI, net banking, or cash on delivery.
What are your delivery hours?
We deliver every day from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM. During peak hours (7 PM – 9 PM), delivery may take an extra 10–15 minutes.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 30–45 minutes. Express delivery (available in select areas) takes 20–30 minutes for an additional fee.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and cash on delivery.
Can I cancel my order after placing it?
You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it at no charge. After that, a cancellation fee may apply if preparation has started.
How do I get a refund for my order?
If your order is incorrect, damaged, or missing items, contact our support team within 1 hour of delivery. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.
Do you support table reservations?
Yes! Visit our Reservation page to book a table. Choose your preferred date, time, seating preference, and any special requests. You'll receive a confirmation with your Reservation ID.
Do I need an account to order?
You can browse the menu without an account, but placing an order requires a Savoro account. Create one for free with your email or phone number.
How do I apply a promo code?
Enter your promo code at checkout in the "Apply Promo Code" field and tap "Apply." The discount will be reflected in your order total before payment.
Is the food quality guaranteed?
Absolutely. Every dish is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients by our certified chefs. If you're unsatisfied, contact us and we'll make it right.
Do you accommodate food allergies?
Yes. Each menu item includes allergen information. You can also add allergy notes in your order. Contact us directly for severe allergies so we can take extra precautions.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, you'll see a real-time tracker on the order confirmation page. You can also use the Track Order link in the footer to check your order status with your Order ID.

Still have questions?

Our support team is available 24/7 to help you with anything.
