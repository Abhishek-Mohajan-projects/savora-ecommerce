  • Orders ₹299–₹498: ₹49 delivery fee

Order Processing

  1. Place your order via the website
  2. Order confirmation notification sent instantly
  3. Kitchen begins preparation (5–10 min)
  4. Rider assigned for pickup
  5. Real-time tracking available
  6. Delivered to your doorstep
🕐

Delivery Hours

  • Monday–Sunday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM
  • Last order accepted: 10:30 PM
  • Express delivery available: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Holiday hours may vary — check the app for updates
⚠️

Important Notes

  • Please provide an accurate delivery address with landmark details
  • Ensure someone is available to receive the order at the door
  • Contactless delivery is available — mention it in the special instructions
  • We cannot deliver to restricted areas, military zones, or P.O. boxes
  • Temperature-sensitive items are packed in insulated containers
