Return & Refund Policy

Your satisfaction matters to us. Here's how we handle returns and refunds.

🤝

Our Commitment

At Savoro, your satisfaction is our top priority. We take every order seriously and stand behind the quality of every dish we prepare. If something isn't right, we'll make it right.

✅

Eligible Refund Cases

You may request a refund if any of the following apply to your order:

  • Order delivered with incorrect items
  • Items significantly different from the menu description
  • Food quality not meeting our stated standards
  • Order delivered cold or in poor condition
  • Missing items from your order
  • Duplicate charges on your payment method
  • Order cancelled by Savoro but not refunded automatically
📝

How to Request a Refund

Follow these simple steps to request a refund:

  • Contact our support team within 1 hour of delivery via phone, email, or live chat
  • Provide your Order ID and a brief description of the issue
  • Attach photos if applicable (for damaged or incorrect items)
  • Our team will review and respond within 24 hours
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days
📦

Incorrect or Damaged Orders

If you receive the wrong order or items are damaged during transit:

  • Do not discard the items
  • Contact us immediately with your Order ID
  • We will arrange a replacement or full refund within 24 hours
  • In some cases, we may ask you to return the items (we'll cover return shipping)
🔍

Missing Items

If any items are missing from your order:

  • Report within 1 hour of delivery
  • We will either deliver the missing items or issue a refund for them
  • No need to return anything for missing item claims
❌

Cancellation Policy

Our cancellation and refund terms based on timing:

  • Within 5 minutes of placing: Full refund, no questions asked
  • 5–15 minutes after placing: 50% refund if preparation hasn't started
  • After preparation has started: No refund available
  • Order rejected by restaurant: Full refund within 24 hours

To cancel, go to My Orders in your profile or contact support immediately.

⏳

Refund Processing Time

Refund timelines vary depending on your payment method:

Payment Method Refund Time
Credit/Debit Card 5–7 business days
UPI / Digital Wallets 24–48 hours
Net Banking 5–10 business days
Cash on Delivery Savoro account credit (instant)
Promo code discounts Returned as account credit
⚠️

Non-Refundable Situations

The following situations are not eligible for a refund:

  • Change of mind after order preparation has started
  • Requests made more than 1 hour after delivery
  • Minor variations in taste or seasoning preferences
  • Orders placed with incorrect address details
  • Promotional or combo orders (refund limited to paid amount)
  • Items consumed partially without prior complaint

Refund requests outside the stated timeframes may be reviewed on a case-by-case basis at Savoro's discretion.

📞

Contact for Refund Requests

Need help with a refund? Reach out to us:

  • Phone: +8801537418504 (Mon–Sun, 10 AM – 11 PM)
  • Email: refunds@savoro.com
  • Live chat on the website during business hours

Please have your Order ID ready for faster assistance.

This policy is effective as of January 1, 2026 and applies to all orders placed through Savoro.
