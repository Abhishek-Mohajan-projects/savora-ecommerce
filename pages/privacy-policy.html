Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: July 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Savoro Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Savoro," "we," "our," "us") operates the Savoro platform, including our website at www.savoro.com, mobile applications, and related food ordering and delivery services (collectively, the "Services"). We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of the personal information we collect from our users, customers, and visitors.

This Privacy Policy describes our practices regarding the collection, use, storage, disclosure, and protection of your personal information when you access or use our Services. It applies to all users, including visitors, registered account holders, merchants, and delivery partners.

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please discontinue use of the Services immediately. Where required by applicable law, we will obtain your explicit consent before collecting or processing your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various categories of information to provide and improve our Services. The specific types of information we collect depend on how you interact with our platform:

  • Identity Information: Full name, date of birth, gender, and profile photograph when you create an account
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, and billing and delivery addresses
  • Account Credentials: Username, encrypted password, and security questions used for authentication
  • Payment Information: Credit or debit card numbers, UPI IDs, wallet details, and billing information processed during checkout
  • Transaction Records: Order history, itemized purchase details, amounts paid, refunds issued, promotional codes applied, and loyalty points earned or redeemed
  • Device and Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device model, unique device identifiers, screen resolution, and language settings
  • Usage and Interaction Data: Pages and menus viewed, items searched or filtered, time spent on pages, items added to cart or wishlist, referral sources, and clickstream patterns
  • Location Information: Delivery addresses you provide and, with your device-level consent, approximate geographic location to suggest nearby restaurants and estimate delivery times
  • Communications: Messages you send to our customer support team, feedback and reviews you submit, and responses to surveys or promotions
  • Preferences and Settings: Dietary restrictions, cuisine preferences, notification settings, language selection, and saved payment methods

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, each grounded in a legitimate business interest, contractual necessity, or your explicit consent:

  • Order Processing and Fulfillment: To accept, confirm, and deliver your food orders, including communicating real-time order status updates and estimated delivery times
  • Account Management: To create and maintain your Savoro account, authenticate your identity, and provide access to order history, saved addresses, and preferences
  • Payment Processing: To facilitate secure payment transactions, process refunds, detect fraudulent activity, and comply with financial regulations
  • Personalization: To tailor menu recommendations, curated collections, and promotional offers based on your ordering history, stated preferences, and browsing behavior
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, resolve disputes, investigate complaints, and provide assistance through our support channels
  • Service Improvement: To analyze aggregate usage patterns, conduct A/B testing, perform quality assurance, and develop new features, menu categories, and delivery capabilities
  • Marketing and Promotions: To send you personalized offers, discount codes, newsletters, and product announcements, subject to your communication preferences and applicable consent requirements
  • Safety and Security: To detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activity; to enforce our Terms of Service; and to protect the rights and safety of Savoro, our users, and the public
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, court orders, and government requests, including tax reporting and food safety record-keeping obligations

4. Payment Information

Your financial security is a top priority. All payment transactions on the Savoro platform are processed through reputable, PCI-DSS Level 1 certified third-party payment gateways. We do not directly collect, store, or have access to your full credit card number, CVV, PIN, or netbanking credentials.

When you save a payment method to your Savoro account, only a tokenized reference (not your actual card details) is retained by our payment processor to enable faster checkout on future orders. All payment data is transmitted over encrypted connections using TLS 1.2 or higher.

We reserve the right to share payment-related information with financial institutions and fraud prevention agencies where necessary to complete a transaction, resolve a dispute, or comply with legal obligations. Our payment processors include but are not limited to Razorpay, Stripe, and PayU, each operating under their own published privacy policies.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, pixel tags, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather analytical insights. These technologies allow us to:

  • Essential Cookies: Maintain your authenticated session, preserve items in your shopping cart, and remember your login credentials across visits
  • Functional Cookies: Store your preferences such as delivery address, language, currency, dietary filters, and dark or light theme selection
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymized data on how visitors navigate the platform, which pages are most visited, and where users encounter errors, helping us optimize performance
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver targeted advertisements relevant to your interests, limit the number of times you see an ad, and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time through our Cookie Consent banner displayed on your first visit, or by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impair the functionality of the Services. For a comprehensive breakdown of the specific cookies we use, their providers, and their retention periods, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

6. Marketing Communications

With your opt-in consent, we may send you marketing and promotional communications through the following channels:

  • Email: Personalized newsletters featuring new menu items, seasonal offers, exclusive discount codes, and curated recommendations based on your preferences
  • SMS and WhatsApp: Time-sensitive notifications about limited-time deals, flash sales, order-ready alerts, and delivery partner arrival notifications
  • Push Notifications: Real-time alerts for price drops on wishlist items, nearby restaurant openings, loyalty reward milestones, and order status changes
  • In-App Messaging: Contextual suggestions and promotional banners displayed within the Savoro application during your browsing sessions

You may withdraw your consent and opt out of any or all marketing communications at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any email, replying "STOP" to any SMS, disabling push notifications in your device settings, or updating your communication preferences within your Savoro account profile. Please allow up to 48 hours for your preferences to take full effect across all systems.

We will continue to send you non-promotional, transactional communications (such as order confirmations, delivery updates, and account security alerts) regardless of your marketing preferences, as these are necessary for the functioning of the Services.

7. Data Security

We employ a multi-layered security architecture designed to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, and destruction:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher protocols
  • Encryption at Rest: Sensitive personal and payment data stored on our servers is encrypted using AES-256 encryption standards
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access policies ensure that only authorized personnel with a legitimate business need can access personal data, with all access logged and audited
  • Infrastructure Security: Our platform is hosted on SOC 2 Type II certified cloud infrastructure with automated threat detection, DDoS mitigation, and continuous monitoring
  • Penetration Testing: We conduct regular third-party security audits, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing to identify and remediate potential weaknesses
  • Incident Response: We maintain a documented data breach response plan and will notify affected users and relevant supervisory authorities within 72 hours of discovering a qualifying breach, as required by applicable law
  • Employee Practices: All employees with access to user data undergo background checks, receive mandatory data protection training, and are bound by confidentiality agreements

While we implement these safeguards, no system is completely immune to security threats. We encourage you to use strong, unique passwords for your Savoro account and to report any suspicious activity to our support team immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

To operate and enhance our Services, we work with a limited number of trusted third-party service providers. We share your information with these partners only to the extent necessary for them to perform their designated functions:

  • Payment Gateways: Razorpay, Stripe, and PayU process payment information to complete transactions and handle chargebacks or disputes
  • Delivery Partners: Authorized delivery personnel and logistics partners receive your name, delivery address, phone number, and order details solely to fulfill and deliver your orders
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics and similar tools receive anonymized usage data to help us understand platform performance, user behavior, and areas for improvement
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Amazon Web Services and similar providers store application data and backups on secure, geographically distributed servers
  • Communication Services: Twilio, SendGrid, and similar providers facilitate transactional emails, SMS notifications, and customer support messaging
  • Fraud Prevention: Specialized fraud detection services analyze transaction patterns to identify and prevent suspicious or fraudulent activity on your account

All third-party partners are bound by contractual data processing agreements that require them to protect your information, use it only for the purposes we specify, and comply with applicable data protection laws. We do not sell your personal information to any third party for their independent marketing purposes.

9. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction and applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, along with information about how it is processed
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information in your account
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal obligations to retain certain records for tax, food safety, or regulatory compliance
  • Right to Restriction: Request that we limit the processing of your data in specific circumstances, such as when you contest its accuracy
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format (such as CSV or JSON) for transfer to another service
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes, including direct marketing and profiling
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw any previously given consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to the withdrawal

To exercise any of these rights, please submit a request to our Data Protection Officer at avimm079@gmail.com. We will respond to all verified requests within 30 days. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing your request to prevent unauthorized access to your data.

10. Children's Privacy

The Savoro platform and its Services are designed for use by individuals who are at least 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, solicit, or maintain personal information from children under the age of 16. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal data without their consent, they should contact us immediately at avimm079@gmail.com.

Upon receiving such notification, we will take prompt steps to investigate and delete any personal information associated with the child's account from our systems. If we are required to obtain verifiable consent under applicable law before collecting data from a child, we will do so using mechanisms compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) or equivalent local regulations.

Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor their children's online activity and to educate them about safe internet practices, including the risks of sharing personal information with unknown platforms.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or operational needs. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Post a prominent notice on our website and within the Savoro application
  • Send an email notification to the address associated with your account
  • Where required by law, obtain your renewed consent before applying the updated policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data. Your continued use of the Services after any modifications to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms. If you do not agree with any changes, you may close your account and discontinue use of the Services.
