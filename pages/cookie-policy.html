Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us recognize your browser, remember your preferences, and improve your browsing experience. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information on their own but can be linked to other data we hold about you.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following categories of cookies: Essential Cookies, Analytics Cookies, Functional Cookies, and Marketing Cookies. Each type serves a specific purpose as described below.

3. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Platform to function properly. They enable core features such as:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Shopping cart functionality
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Load balancing and server management

These cookies cannot be disabled as the Platform will not work without them.

4. Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our Platform by collecting and reporting information anonymously. We use:

Cookie Purpose Retention
Google Analytics (_ga, _gid) Website usage analysis 2 years
Hotjar (_hj*) User behavior tracking and heatmaps 1 year
Internal analytics Page views, session duration, navigation patterns Session

5. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization:

  • Language and region preferences
  • Display settings (dark/light theme)
  • Recently viewed items
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Form auto-fill data

They remember your choices to provide a more personalized experience.

6. Marketing Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests:

Cookie Purpose Retention
Facebook Pixel (_fbp) Ad tracking and conversion measurement 90 days
Google Ads (_gcl_au) Ad campaign effectiveness 90 days
Retargeting cookies Showing relevant ads on other websites 30 days

These cookies track your browsing across websites and may be set by third-party advertising partners.

7. Managing Cookie Preferences

You can manage cookies through:

  • Your browser settings (block or delete cookies)
  • Our cookie consent banner (shown on first visit)
  • Opt-out links provided by third-party services

Note: Disabling essential cookies may impair Platform functionality.

8. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services embedded in our Platform:

  • Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe)
  • Analytics providers (Google, Hotjar)
  • Social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram)
  • Advertising networks

We do not control third-party cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

9. Cookie Retention

Cookie lifespans vary:

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for a set period (see individual cookie descriptions above)
  • Most cookies expire after 1 month to 2 years

You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through our Platform or via email.
